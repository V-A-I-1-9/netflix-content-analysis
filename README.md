# Netflix Content Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Netflixâ€™s movies and TV shows dataset to generate insights
that can help Netflix decide what type of content to produce and how to grow its
business in different countries.

## Dataset
The dataset contains information about Netflix content including:
- Type (Movie / TV Show)
- Title
- Director
- Cast
- Country
- Release Year
- Rating
- Duration
- Genre

## Objectives
- Analyze content distribution across movies and TV shows
- Understand content trends over time
- Identify dominant genres and countries
- Generate data-driven business insights and recommendations

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab

## Key Insights
- Movies dominate the platform, but TV shows have increased in recent years
- The United States and India contribute the most content
- Drama and International genres are highly prevalent
- Netflix focuses largely on mature audience content

## Business Recommendations
- Increase investment in TV shows
- Expand content production in high-performing regions
- Focus on popular genres while maintaining content diversity
